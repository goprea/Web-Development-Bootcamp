Who am I?


I'm Gabriela (she/her) based in Leeds, UK. A jack of all trades, aspiring to be good at everything and knowing all there is to know. Ever annoyed at being boxed in, I spend my time pursuing cross-discipline endeavours. Since I was a little Gabbie, arts & crafts have been my biggest passions, trying a new drawing/painting medium, watching tutorials online on how to use Photoshop and how to craft my own room decorations = a never-ending saga. In May 2022, my curiosity led me down the path of doing a Web Development Bootcamp with Bath Spa University which has been the best decision I've ever taken. I realised that my middle name should be "Web Developer and Ui/UX Designer" (it's quite a long middle name, I know).

I may not have years of experience, however, my hunger to learn and develop the best version of myself will not disappoint any of us. Adaptability, flexibility and quick grasping of new systems and processes are the aces up my sleeves. Of course, we cannot overlook the fact that I am a great class clown (according to recent research this means it's a sign of high intelligence; research said that, not me).

Thanks for reading. Get in touch with me! I will be a great asset to your team and company.
